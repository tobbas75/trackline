# Molex 209142-0180 — LTE Flexible PCB Antenna

## Summary

Flexible PCB monopole antenna for 4G/LTE covering 698-4000 MHz. Adhesive-mount with 180mm coaxial cable and U.FL/MHF connector. Intended as the cellular antenna for the EG800Q-EU modem daughter card.

**Action required:** Confirm efficiency from full datasheet. Low-band efficiency is the key concern for Band 28 performance in remote areas.

## Ground Plane Requirements

Not specified in available distributor data. General guidance for flex PCB antennas:
- Requires a ground plane on the host PCB (the modem daughter card in this design)
- Minimum ground plane: ~50 x 30 mm recommended for low-band performance
- Antenna element should extend beyond the PCB edge (not over the ground plane)
- Keep-out zone: no copper pour under the antenna element
- Cable routing should avoid sharp bends near the connector

**Action required:** The modem daughter card is only 30 x 25 mm. This may be insufficient ground plane for good 700 MHz performance. Review Molex application note and consider whether the main board ground plane (90 x 70 mm) contributes when connected via the 20-pin header. A pigtail to an external SMA bulkhead antenna may perform better in the field.

## Application Notes for This Design

1. **Mounting location:** Inside the IP67 polycarbonate enclosure, adhered to the enclosure wall. Polycarbonate is RF-transparent at these frequencies. Avoid mounting near metal features or battery pack.

2. **Cable routing:** 180mm cable length is adequate to route from modem daughter card to enclosure wall. Avoid running parallel to power traces.

3. **Connector:** U.FL mates with the J_ANT connector on the modem daughter card (LCSC C88374).

4. **Temperature:** Enclosure may reach 70 C in tropical sun (per DESIGN_SPEC). Verify antenna adhesive and materials are rated for this.
